## Honest stage assessment

TerraRun is a **working MVP**, not a mature product with a million-user base:

- Some features (AI coach, organizer dashboard) are in **demo / beta**.
- Infrastructure scaling and content moderation are tasks before mass marketing.
- Open to **pilot partnerships** with limited scope and measurable results in 4–8 weeks.
