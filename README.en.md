## Product at a glance

TerraRun combines **map, training, social network, and monetization** in a single iOS app:

- **Territory capture** — a closed GPS route becomes a polygon on the map; zones can overlap and transfer between runners.
- **Training log** — plans, trends, HealthKit, running calculators.
- **Community** — feed, clubs, events, chats, trail flags (run traces on the map).
- **World Journey** — meta-quest from Moscow to Vladivostok based on accumulated distance.
- **AI coach “Mark Terry”** — personal plans and workout analysis (on the roadmap).

**Platforms:** iOS 17+ (SwiftUI) · cloud API · [terrarun.ru](https://terrarun.ru)
